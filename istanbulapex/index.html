Welcome to Apex Hotel Istanbul

Istanbul Apex Hotel is a historical building with character in the SULTANAHMET district. The hotel’s super location offers various numbers of historical attractions, all in walking distance.

World famous Grand Bazaar are just steps away, while many more historical monuments and relics remained from Byzantium and Ottoman Empires such as Obelisk; Underground Cistern; Hippodrome; the world’s only six minareted mosque, Blue Mosque; new Seven Wonders of World candidate, Hagia Sophia; Turkish & Islamic Art Museum; Mosaic Museum; Topkapi Palace... only in 5-minute walking distance.

Istanbul Apex Hotel, Turkish hospitality and high quality service standards and a modern hotel decorated to carry ID. All 15 of our room air conditioning, minibar, television, bath-tub, hair dryer can be found.

Prime Location

The Boutique Apex Hotel is centrally located in the heart of the old city in the popular Sultanahmet area. The hotel is just a short distance from Sultanahmet Square, Aya Sofia, the Blue Mosque, Topkapi Palace, the spice market, the underground cistern and the hippodrome.
